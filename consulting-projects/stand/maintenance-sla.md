## 1. Overview

This is an optional, ongoing maintenance and support agreement available to Client following completion of the sprint engagement. This addendum is not required and does not affect the terms of the main Independent Contractor Agreement.

## 2. Services Included

- **Bug fixes** — Defects in functionality delivered under the original scope (Section 1 of the main agreement)
- **Minor updates** — Small UI tweaks, copy changes, configuration adjustments, and minor improvements (under 2 hours of work each)
- **Monitoring** — Contractor will monitor production uptime and third-party service health (Vercel, Supabase, PostHog) during business hours
- **Priority support** — 12-hour response time for critical issues (app down, data loss, broken core flows); 48-hour response time for non-critical issues

## 3. Excluded from Retainer

- New feature development (scoped and quoted separately)
- Work exceeding 10 hours in a calendar month (overage billed at $150/hr with prior approval)
- Third-party service costs (Vercel, Supabase, Printify, PostHog, etc.)
- Issues caused by Client modifications to the codebase

## 4. Compensation

**$2,000 USD per month**, billed on the 1st of each month, due within 5 business days of invoice.

## 5. Term & Cancellation

- This agreement begins on a mutually agreed start date following delivery of the sprint.
- Either party may cancel with **30 days written notice** via email.
- No minimum commitment — Client may activate or cancel at any time with proper notice.
- Unused hours do not roll over between months.

## 6. Communication

- Bug reports and support requests must be submitted via email to Contractor's address listed in the main agreement.
- Critical issues (app down, data loss) may also be reported via text message to Contractor's phone number.
- Contractor will acknowledge all requests within the applicable response window (12 hours critical, 48 hours non-critical).

## 7. Relationship to Main Agreement

All other terms of the main Independent Contractor Agreement remain in effect, including Sections 10 (Limitation of Liability), 11 (Independent Contractor), 12 (Confidentiality), and 13 (Dispute Resolution).
