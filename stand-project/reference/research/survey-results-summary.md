# Final Survey Results — 100 Families

Source: SurveyMonkey quantitative survey (n=100 parents)

---

## Interest Level

| Response | % |
|---|---|
| Very interested | 42% |
| Somewhat interested | 31% |
| Neutral | 17% |
| Not very interested | 5% |
| Not interested at all | 5% |

**73% interested or very interested.**

## Why Enroll? (Select all)

| Reason | % |
|---|---|
| Teaching financial literacy | 71% |
| Building confidence and social skills | 67% |
| Earning potential for child | 53% |
| Developing business acumen early | 49% |
| Productive alternative to screen time | 45% |
